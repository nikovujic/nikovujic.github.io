About the editor

Welcome to my website! My name is Niko Vujić, and I'm a mechanical engineering student with an interest in robotics, electronics, and software.






How has this page come to be?

I have always used a physical notebook for logging everything in my life, but as time has passed and the projects got bigger and bigger (and more and more digital), the notebook just couldn't cut it anymore. In an attempt to document my projects more efficiently (and digitally), it occurred to me that if it's digital, why wouldn't it be public? That is the main point of this website – to document my personal projects, and if anyone wants to replicate them, they are welcome to do so. This will be a hub with all of my ideas and works in progress. There should be a lot of stuff here, so I hope that you'll stick around!
I invite you to explore my portfolio, where you'll find detailed descriptions, images, and documentation of my projects. Feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions or suggestions.
